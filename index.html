Workflow

How the full FortiFocus system works

1
Create or Reopen Your Account

Start with the authentication flow, validate your details, and route admins through their own secure entry.

2
Set Goals and Queue Tasks

Define a weekly goal, line up your next tasks, and give each one time, duration, due date, and priority.

3
Start a Guided Focus Session

Choose a preset or custom duration, select study music, and move into the timer or full-screen focus mode.

4
Log the Outcome Automatically

Completed and interrupted sessions are stored with subject, planned time, actual time, and timestamps.

5
Review Reflections and Streaks

Add notes, blockers, and next steps, then check the streak board and monthly calendar to protect momentum.

6
Export or Administer

Generate a study report for one user or export all sessions to CSV from the admin dashboard.
